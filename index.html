The team has been assembled by recruiting through several platforms: connections of the founders, physical posters on strategic places like universities or libraries, announcements on LinkedIn, Twitter, Instragram, etc. Also, a lot of our members came directly from The Sandbox Official Discord. Here is where we also met and started building a relationship with our virtual landlords. It is worth noting that despite this phase being mostly done, the team is constantly changing, and we are still recruiting. The team is diverse and multi-national, we have members in Spain, United Kingdom, United States, Switzerland, The Netherlands, Colombia, Argentina, Costa Rica, India and Australia.

Strategic Partnerships with other relevant ventures to either delegate certain areas of the project or to enjoy a mutual benefit of some sort like visibility We are working with Brikken to implement asset tokenizaction and about to close a deal with Ark Digital Agency to implement and scale a marketing strategy. Additionally, not long ago 8-BitMafia (NFT collection) and Mafiaverse merged. This provides a lot of value to the community as both projects are intimately related by theme.
